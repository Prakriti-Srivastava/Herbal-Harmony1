Yoga Beyond One Path

Yoga has many forms – movement, breath, meditation, and devotion. Each path is different, yet all lead to balance and inner peace.

YOGA FOR CHILDRENS


Yoga for children (5–12 years) helps improve flexibility, focus, and confidence while making their body strong and mind calm in a playful way

YOGA FOR TEENAGERS


Yoga for teenagers (13–19 years) helps reduce stress, improve posture, boost confidence, and maintain physical fitness during growth years.

YOGA FOR ADULTS

Prone yoga poses, done while lying on the stomach, strengthen the back, improve posture, and energize the body.

YOGA FOR MIDDLE AGE

Supine yoga poses, practiced while lying on the back, promote relaxation, improve flexibility, and support deep breathing.

BALANCING POSE

Inversion yoga poses, where the heart is above the head, enhance circulation, build strength, and improve focus and balance.
