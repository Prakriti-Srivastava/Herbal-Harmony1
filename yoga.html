Yoga Beyond One Path

Yoga has many forms – movement, breath, meditation, and devotion. Each path is different, yet all lead to balance and inner peace.

STANDING POSES


Standing yoga poses improve balance, strength, and focus while grounding the body.

SITTING POSE


Sitting yoga poses enhance flexibility, calm the mind, and support meditation and breathing practices.

PRONE POSE

Prone yoga poses, done while lying on the stomach, strengthen the back, improve posture, and energize the body.
