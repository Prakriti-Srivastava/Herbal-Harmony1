Understanding Cold Symptoms

Colds are caused by viruses and can lead to a variety of symptoms, including:

  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Congestion
  • Sneezing
  • Low-grade fever

It's important to recognize these symptoms early for effective treatment.

Preventive Measures

To reduce the risk of catching a cold, consider the following preventive measures:

  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water.
  • Avoid close contact with individuals who are sick.
  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids.
  • Get adequate rest to support your immune system.

When to Seek Medical Attention

If you experience severe symptoms or if your cold lasts for more than 10 days, it's important to seek medical attention. Additionally, if you have difficulty breathing, chest pain, or a high fever, contact a healthcare professional immediately.
