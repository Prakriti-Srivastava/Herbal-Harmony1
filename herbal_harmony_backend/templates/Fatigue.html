Understanding Fatigue Symptoms

Fatigue affects both the body and mind. It's more than just feeling tired — it's a constant feeling of exhaustion that interferes with daily activities.:

  • Constant Tiredness
  • Lack of Concentration
  • Sleep Problems
  • Shortness of Breath (in severe fatigue)
  • Mood Changes

It's important to recognize these symptoms early for effective treatment.

Preventive Measures

If fatigue continues for more than two weeks, or is accompanied by weight loss, high fever, or depression, it may indicate an underlying health condition — seek medical advice.

  • Avoid caffeine or energy drinks in excess.
  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule.
  • Take breaks between work and screen use.
  • If fatigue continues for more than 2 weeks, consult a doctor.
