Understanding Diabetes Symptoms

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). It can lead to a variety of symptoms, including:

  • Increased thirst and urination
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores or frequent infections
  • Numbness or tingling in the hands and feet

It's important to recognize these symptoms early for effective treatment.
