SHOULDER AND NECK RELEASE YOGA

Shoulder and Neck Release Yoga is a gentle relaxation practice that helps reduce stress, shoulder tightness, neck stiffness, and tension-related discomfort. Slow movement with calm breathing supports body relaxation.

Yoga Category

Shoulder and Neck Relaxation Yoga

Related practices: Shoulder Rolls, Neck Stretch, Calm Breathing

Steps to Perform Shoulder and Neck Release Yoga:

  1. Sit comfortably with your back straight and shoulders relaxed.
  2. Slowly roll your shoulders forward in a circular motion.
  3. Repeat the movement backward gently.
  4. Now slowly tilt your neck toward the right shoulder.
  5. Return to the center and repeat on the left side.
  6. Take slow deep breaths during the practice.
  7. Relax your body and continue for 5–10 minutes.

Benefits of Shoulder and Neck Release Yoga:

  • Helps reduce shoulder and neck tension.
  • Supports relaxation during stress-related headache.
  • Improves gentle flexibility and posture comfort.
  • Promotes calm breathing and relaxation.
  • May help reduce body heaviness and stiffness.

Beginner Tips

Keep all movements slow and comfortable.

Avoid fast neck rotations or forceful stretching.

Breathe calmly throughout the practice.

Stop the practice if you feel pain or discomfort.
