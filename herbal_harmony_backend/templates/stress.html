Understanding Stress Symptoms

Stress affects the mind and body. Common symptoms include:

  • Headache and fatigue
  • Restlessness and irritability
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Loss of focus and motivation
  • Muscle tension or body pain
  • Increased heart rate

Recognizing stress early helps in managing it effectively.

Preventive Measures

To reduce stress naturally:

  • Practice yoga & meditation daily
  • Maintain healthy sleep habits
  • Spend time in nature
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Take breaks during work
  • Avoid overthinking & screen overload
  • Stay hydrated

When to Seek Medical Attention

If stress causes severe symptoms like panic attacks, long-term insomnia, chest pain, or inability to function normally, seek medical help immediately.
