ANULOM VILOM
(ALTERNATE NOSTRIL BREATHING)

Anulom Vilom is a powerful pranayama technique that balances the left and right channels of the body, calms the mind, reduces stress, and improves lung capacity. It enhances overall breathing efficiency and brings mental clarity.

Sanskrit Meaning

Anulom = With the grain / Natural order

Vilom = Against the grain / Reverse order

Meaning: “Alternate Breathing in a Natural Rhythm”

Steps to Perform Anulom Vilom:

  1. Sit comfortably in Sukhasana or Padmasana with a straight spine.
  2. Use your right hand in Nasagra Mudra (thumb + ring finger).
  3. Close your right nostril with your thumb.
  4. Inhale slowly through your left nostril.
  5. Close the left nostril with your ring finger.
  6. Open the right nostril and exhale slowly.
  7. Again inhale through the right nostril.
  8. Close it and exhale through the left nostril.
  9. This completes one round—practice for 5–10 minutes.

Benefits of Anulom Vilom:

  • Reduces stress, anxiety, and mental tension
  • Enhances lung capacity and oxygen flow
  • Balances left-right brain hemispheres
  • Improves focus, memory, and emotional stability
  • Helps in high blood pressure management
  • Purifies the respiratory system
  • Promotes relaxation and calmness

Beginner Tips

Breathe slowly and naturally—do not force the breath.

Practice on an empty stomach for best results.

Keep shoulders relaxed and spine straight.

Start with 5 minutes and gradually increase to 10–15 minutes.
