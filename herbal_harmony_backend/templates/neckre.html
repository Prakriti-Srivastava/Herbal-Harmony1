NECK RELAXATION AND GENTLE STRETCH

Neck Relaxation and Gentle Stretch is a calming yoga practice that may help reduce tension, stiffness, heaviness, and stress around the neck area. Gentle stretching with calm breathing supports relaxation and posture comfort.

Yoga Category

Gentle Neck Relaxation and Stretching

Related practices: Neck Stretch, Greeva Sanchalan, Calm Breathing

Steps to Perform Neck Relaxation and Gentle Stretch:

  1. Sit comfortably with your back straight and shoulders relaxed.
  2. Slowly lower your chin toward your chest while breathing normally.
  3. Gently raise your neck upward without forcing movement.
  4. Slowly turn your neck toward the right side and hold for a few seconds.
  5. Return to the center and repeat on the left side.
  6. Relax your shoulders and continue slow breathing.
  7. Repeat the gentle movements for 5–7 minutes.

Benefits of Neck Relaxation and Gentle Stretch:

  • Helps reduce neck stiffness and tension.
  • Supports relaxation during stress-related headache.
  • Improves gentle neck flexibility.
  • Promotes calm breathing and body relaxation.
  • May help reduce heaviness around the neck and head.

Beginner Tips

Move your neck slowly and gently without jerking.

Stop immediately if you feel pain or dizziness.

Keep your shoulders relaxed throughout the practice.

Calm breathing may improve the relaxation effect.
