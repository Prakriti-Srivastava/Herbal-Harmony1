DEEP BREATHING PRACTICE

Deep Breathing Practice is a calming yoga breathing exercise that may help reduce anxious tension, stress, panic, and restlessness. Slow and steady breathing supports relaxation and emotional balance.

Yoga Guidance

Relaxation and Calm Breathing Practice

Related practices: Deep Breathing, Calm Sitting, Relaxed Breathing

Steps to Perform Deep Breathing Practice:

  1. Sit comfortably in a quiet place with your back straight.
  2. Relax your shoulders and place your hands gently on your knees.
  3. Close your eyes and slowly inhale through your nose.
  4. Take a slow deep breath and allow your chest and abdomen to relax.
  5. Exhale gently and evenly without force.
  6. Focus on calm and steady breathing for several minutes.
  7. Continue the practice for 5–10 minutes.

Benefits of Deep Breathing Practice:

  • May help reduce stress and anxious tension.
  • Supports calmness and emotional balance.
  • Promotes steady and mindful breathing.
  • May reduce feelings of panic and restlessness.
  • Supports relaxation and mental wellness naturally.

Beginner Tips

Keep your breathing slow, soft, and comfortable.

Avoid forceful breathing or overexertion.

Practice in a calm and quiet environment for better relaxation.

Stop the practice if you feel dizzy or uncomfortable.
