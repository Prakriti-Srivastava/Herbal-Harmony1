Culinary Herbs

A culinary herb is a plant used to add flavor, aroma, or color to food, commonly used in cooking and seasoning dishes. Here are some commonly used Culinary herbs and their benefits:

Coriander

Calming fragrance ,Used for stress relief, sleep improvement, perfumes, oils.


Oregano

Strong, woody aroma,Used in cooking, hair growth oils, memory enhancement


Chives

Sweet, spicy smell,Used in cooking, essential oils, stress relief


Sage

Earthy scent,Used for cleansing rituals, teas, air purification


Bay Leaves

Bay leaves are known for their distinctive fragrance and are commonly used in cooking to add flavor to soups, stews, and sauces.


Curry Leaves

Curry leaf is an aromatic herb used in cooking to add flavor and aroma, obtained from the curry tree.


Thyme

Thyme is a fragrant culinary herb used to add flavor to dishes and is known for its antibacterial and medicinal properties


Tarragon

Tarragon is a fragrant culinary herb with a slightly sweet, licorice-like flavor commonly used in French cooking.


Parsley

Parsley is a fresh, mildly flavored culinary herb used for seasoning and garnishing dishes


Fennel Leaves(Saunf)

They are aromatic, feathery green herbs with a mild sweet flavor, used in cooking for seasoning and garnishing.
