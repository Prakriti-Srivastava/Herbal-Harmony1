Aromatic Herbs

Aromatic herbs are plants that contain strong-smelling essential oils and release a pleasant fragrance, commonly used in aromatherapy, cooking, skincare, and natural healing due to their soothing and therapeutic properties. Here are some commonly used Aromatic herbs and their benefits:

Lemon Grass

Calming fragrance ,Used for stress relief, sleep improvement, perfumes, oils.


Rosemary

Strong, woody aroma,Used in cooking, hair growth oils, memory enhancement


Basil

Sweet, spicy smell,Used in cooking, essential oils, stress relief


Sage

Earthy scent,Used for cleansing rituals, teas, air purification


Lavender

Lavender is known for its relaxing aroma and is commonly used in aromatherapy to reduce stress and promote better sleep.


Peppermint

Peppermint is used to relieve digestive discomfort, headaches, and muscle pain due to its cooling and soothing properties.


Chamomile

Chamomile is known for its calming effects and is often used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.


Eucalyptus

Echinacea is known for its immune-boosting properties and is often used to prevent colds and respiratory infections.
