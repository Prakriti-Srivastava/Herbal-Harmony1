"Herbs have been an essential part of human life for centuries, offering healing, flavor, and fragrance. On this page, we explore different types of herbs based on their uses in medicine, cooking, and daily wellness."


Herbs can be broadly categorized into Medicinal Herbs that heal, Aromatic Herbs that refresh, and Culinary Herbs that enhance flavors. Each type plays a unique role in supporting health and lifestyle.


From soothing fevers and boosting immunity to adding aroma in oils and flavor in meals, herbs are nature's versatile gift. Let's discover their types and benefits.

Medicinal Herb

Medicinal herbs are plants that have therapeutic properties and are used in traditional medicine.

Culinary Herbs

Culinary herbs are plants used to flavor dishes and enhance their taste.


Aromatic Herbs

Aromatic herbs are plants that are valued for their fragrant properties and are often used in cooking, perfumery, and traditional medicine.
