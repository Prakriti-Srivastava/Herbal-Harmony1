WARM STEAM INHALATION

Warm Steam Inhalation is a natural home remedy that helps relieve cold, cough, blocked nose, and sinus congestion. The warm steam helps loosen mucus and makes breathing easier.

Natural Therapy

Warm Steam Therapy

steam = warm vapor from hot water

Steps to Perform Warm Steam Inhalation:

  1. Boil clean water and pour it carefully into a large bowl.
  2. Sit comfortably near the bowl and keep your face at a safe distance.
  3. Cover your head with a towel to trap the steam.
  4. Close your eyes and inhale the steam slowly through your nose and mouth.
  5. Continue for 5–10 minutes while breathing gently.
  6. After completion, relax and drink warm water if needed.

Benefits of Warm Steam Inhalation:

  • Relieves blocked nose and congestion.
  • Helps reduce cold and cough symptoms.
  • Loosens mucus and improves breathing.
  • Provides relaxation and comfort.
  • Helps soothe throat irritation and dryness.

Beginner Tips

Do not keep your face too close to hot water because it may cause burns.

You can add tulsi leaves, ajwain, or eucalyptus oil for extra relief.

Always use steam carefully, especially for children.

If you feel uncomfortable or dizzy, stop immediately and rest.

Steam inhalation can be used 1–2 times daily during cold or congestion.
