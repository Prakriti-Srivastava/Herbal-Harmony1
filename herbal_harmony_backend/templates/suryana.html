SURYA NAMASKAR (SUN SALUTATION)

Surya Namaskar is a dynamic sequence of 12 powerful yoga postures. It enhances flexibility, boosts blood circulation, energizes the body, and balances both mind and breath. It is considered a complete full-body workout.

Sanskrit Meaning

Surya = Sun

Namaskar = Salutation

Meaning: “Honoring the Sun with movement and breath”

12 Steps of Surya Namaskar:

  1. Pranamasana (Prayer Pose) – Stand straight, bring feet together. Join palms at the chest and relax your whole body while breathing deeply.
  2. Hasta Uttanasana (Raised Arms Pose) – Inhale. Raise both arms upward and take a slight backward bend while keeping the spine long.
  3. Hasta Padasana (Standing Forward Bend) – Exhale. Bend forward from the hips, keeping the spine straight. Touch hands to the floor or your legs comfortably.
  4. Ashwa Sanchalanasana (Equestrian Pose) – Inhale. Take your right leg back, bend the left knee, keep fingertips on the floor, chest lifted.
  5. Dandasana (Plank Pose) – Exhale. Take your left leg back. Bring the body into a straight line from head to heels.
  6. Ashtanga Namaskara (Eight-Limbed Salutation) – Exhale. Lower knees, chest, and chin down to the floor. Keep hips slightly lifted.
  7. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose) – Inhale. Slide forward and raise your chest. Keep elbows bent and shoulders relaxed.
  8. Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward Dog Pose) – Exhale. Lift hips up to form an inverted V-shape. Keep spine long and heels gently pressing downward.
  9. Ashwa Sanchalanasana – Inhale. Bring the left foot forward between the hands. Keep right leg extended back and chest open.
  10. Hasta Padasana – Exhale. Bring the right foot forward and fold your body down comfortably.
  11. Hasta Uttanasana – Inhale. Raise arms up and gently bend backward, stretching your entire body upward.
  12. Pranamasana – Exhale. Return to the standing position with palms joined at the chest.

Benefits of Surya Namaskar:

  • Improves flexibility and posture
  • Enhances blood circulation
  • Boosts metabolism and energy levels
  • Strengthens muscles and joints
  • Supports weight loss naturally
  • Reduces stress and improves focus
  • Improves digestive and respiratory health
  • Enhances body balance & stamina

Beginner Tips

Perform slowly and maintain proper form.

Start with 3–5 rounds, gradually increasing to 12.

Sync breath with each posture.

Best practiced on an empty stomach.
