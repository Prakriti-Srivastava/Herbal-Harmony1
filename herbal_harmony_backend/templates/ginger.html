GINGER KADHA

Ginger kadha may provide warm supportive care during cold, fever, weakness, and throat discomfort. It may help the body feel more comfortable when combined with hydration and rest.

Herbal Warm Support

Warm Herbal Recovery Support

Related support: Ginger, Warm Fluids, Herbal Care, Recovery Support

Steps to Prepare Ginger Kadha:

  1. Boil one glass of water in a pan.
  2. Add freshly crushed ginger into the water.
  3. Allow the mixture to boil for several minutes.
  4. Strain the kadha carefully into a cup.
  5. Drink warm in a relaxed environment.
  6. Rest properly and stay hydrated.

Benefits of Ginger Kadha:

  • May provide warmth and comfort.
  • Supports hydration and recovery.
  • May help improve throat comfort.
  • Supports rest and relaxation during illness.

Beginner Tips

Drink the kadha warm, not extremely hot.

Rest properly and drink enough fluids.

Avoid heavy oily food during fever and weakness.

If symptoms become severe or continue, seek medical advice.
