Understanding Fever Symptoms

Fever is a common symptom of various illnesses and can lead to a variety of symptoms, including:

  • High body temperature (above 102°F)
  • Body Pain and Fatigue
  • Headache
  • Cold, Cough, and Sore Throat
  • Chills and Shivering
  • Weakness and Drowsiness

It's important to recognize these symptoms early for effective treatment.

Preventive Measures

To reduce the risk of catching Fever, consider the following preventive measures:

  • Maintain Proper Hygiene.
  • Eat a Balanced, Healthy Diet.
  • Stay Hydrated.
  • Avoid Sudden Temperature Changes.
  • Keep a Safe Distance from Sick People.
  • Strengthen Immunity Naturally.
  • Practice Yoga & Meditation.
  • Keep Your Body Active.

When to Seek Medical Attention

If you experience severe symptoms or if your fever lasts for more than 3-4 days, it's important to seek medical attention. Additionally, if you have difficulty breathing, chest pain, or a high fever, contact a healthcare professional immediately.
